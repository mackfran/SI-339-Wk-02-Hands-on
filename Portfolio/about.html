About Me

I'm a senior at the University of Michigan completing a Bachelor of Science in Information. As a student in the School of Information, I completed courses within the information analysis and social media specialist paths. After graduation, I will be working for Ford Motor Copany as an Information Technology Ford College Graduate (IT FCG) where I will complete three rotations in three years. My first rotation will be as a Transformation Analyst, where I will work with a team of agile coaches to transform the way a set of software engineering teams works.

Outside of the classroom, I'm a social media editorial and analytics intern for UMSocial. I serve as a core member of EnCore, a multicultural, co-ed, hip hop dance group. I'm also a member of the Gamma Phi Beta Sorority, where I previously served as the Education Vice President.

I hope you'll stick around to learn more about the projects I've worked on and what I've accomplished throughout my undergraduate career.
